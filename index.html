About me

My goal is to create designs that are not only eye-catching but also effectively convey messages and leave a lasting impression. If you’re curious about my work or would like to collaborate, feel free to explore my site and reach out!

Packaging design

The packaging design combines modern simplicity with a touch of tradition. The landscape illustration evokes a natural, serene feel, while the color scheme—deep red for Kékfrankos and fresh green for Olasz Rizling—clearly distinguishes the two wine types. The practical, easy-to-carry design with an integrated tap ensures convenience, while the overall aesthetic is clean, elegant, and visually appealing, reflecting the quality and natural origins of the product.

Logo design

The "Könyvmoly Kuckó" logo blends elegance and warmth, using classic serif typography for "Könyvmoly" and a playful script for "kuckó." The green color palette symbolizes tranquility, while the book-inspired emblem adds a creative, inviting touch, perfectly reflecting the brand's focus on reading and a cozy environment.
